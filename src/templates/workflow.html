Secure GWAS Workflow

These are the steps you'll need to follow to securely run GWAS on our portal

1

Prepare Data

Save the GWAS data in standard VCF format in a google cloud bucket for the project where you will want the computation to take place.

2

Create or Join Project

Go to the "Projects" page and either create or join a Secure GWAS project with the parameters and parties that you want.

3

All Parties Join Project

Coordinate with all parties to join the project and confirm that your configuration works for everyone.

4

Begin Workflow

Go to the project page and click the "ready" button to indicate that you're ready to begin the workflow.

5

Wait for Readiness

Once everyone has indicated readiness, the workflow will automatically begin. You can watch the progress in the portal.

6

Enjoy the Results

Download the results and analyze them to your heart's content!
